5. The Profile

The personal Profile is the heart piece of The Hospitality Club. Here, every member gives information about him/herself so other members can contact him/her or decide if they would like to host him/her. Besides personal information, information about the travels and interests and the help offered by a member, the Profile contains another major feature: the comments. As a member, you can comment on any other member that you personally know, and these comments will appear in the Profile. Also, you can specify if you trust a member, or if you have been his/her host or guest. This feature adds security and fun for all members.

When you sign up, please remember that your Profile will be the basis for all your activities within The Hospitality Club, so take a minute and tell as much about yourself as you would like to know about another person that was coming to stay with you.
